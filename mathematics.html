Mathematics

Mathematics is not about speed or tricks. It is the study of structure, relationships, and logical necessity. REAL Learning teaches mathematics from first principles.


Class 5 Mathematics

Numbers, factors, multiples, fractions, basic geometry, and introduction to logical reasoning through patterns.

Class 6 Mathematics

Natural numbers, integers, ratios, basic algebra, angles, symmetry, and data handling.

Class 7 Mathematics

Rational numbers, linear expressions, congruence, practical geometry, and proportional reasoning.

Class 8 Mathematics

Linear equations, squares & cubes, mensuration, coordinate geometry, and visual proof methods.

Class 9 Mathematics

Number systems, polynomials, Euclidean geometry, coordinate geometry, statistics, and proof-based thinking.

Class 10 Mathematics

Real numbers, quadratic equations, trigonometry, coordinate geometry, calculus intuition, and probability.
